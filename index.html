Plant General Safety Guidelines

  1. Always wear mandatory PPE (Helmet, Safety Shoes, Ear Plugs, etc.) inside plant.
  2. Follow all safety instructions, signages and SOPs.
  3. Do not operate any machine without proper training and authorization.
  4. Report all unsafe acts, unsafe conditions and near misses immediately.
  5. Maintain proper housekeeping at workplace.
  6. Do not enter restricted or hazardous areas without permission.
  7. Follow Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedure before maintenance work.
  8. No smoking, alcohol or tobacco inside plant premises.
  9. Use correct tools and equipment for the job.
  10. Follow emergency procedures and assembly point instructions.
  11. Immediately report any injury or incident to supervisor.
  12. Always follow work permit system (Hot work, Height work, Confined space etc.).
